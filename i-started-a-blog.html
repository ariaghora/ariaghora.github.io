I started a blog

I know that blogging takes time. I know that it is not easy to stay committed to writing. Nonetheless, I feel something missing if I have no personal development blog, as I am involved in many projects, as well as my personal projects. Furthermore, writing what I've learned tends to make me understand them better.

Scope

I want to stay focused and relevant. Software development and machine learning are my strong suit (I mean, relatively stronger than any others of mine). Thus, mostly I will talk about them, even though it is also possible to extend the scope, such as my personal opinion about more general topics.

language

Mostly I will write in English, since the topics in the scope of my blog are discussed in English. It is not my first language, but I hope anyone can understand. Sometimes I will probably write in Bahasa Indonesia (my native language) if needed.
